    Welcome to Xianzu’s Homepage!!

    I am a senior majoring in Data Science and Big Data Technology at Yangtze University and have been fortunate to be mentored by Prof. Guozhong Gao . and I am fortunate that I am now an intern at ENCODE Lab@Westlake University under the guidance of Prof. Huan Wang .

    My current research interests are in Computer Vision. In particular, I am interested in the applied aspects of 3D AIGC, 3D reconstruction,3D Video Generation(3D Asset),Federated Learning , and I am eager to explore the intersection of cognitively inspired 3DV and AI's ability to "imagine" and "dream" .

    If you are interested in my research, please feel free to email me.
